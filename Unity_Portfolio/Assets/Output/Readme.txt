Magic Hit Vol.1

- a Particle Cook Production.

How to preview?
   Run the MagicHitEffectVol1 scene and you can preview those effects by switching the tabs.

How to use?
	You just need to import the folder of the effect you need into your project.

How to play those effects?
	Instantiate them in game. For those non-loop effects you should destroy them yourself in code.
	Rest of them will auto destroyed after all particles were stopped.
